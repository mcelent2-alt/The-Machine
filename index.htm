Abstract

This paper examines whether the documented history of VXM Technologies, MetaQor, and ExQor Technologies is consistent with the operational profile of a company embedded in classified or semi-classified research programs. The evidence strongly supports the hypothesis that these entities functioned as a persistent, low-visibility R&D conduit for sensitive systems across computation, cognition, and biological substrates.
